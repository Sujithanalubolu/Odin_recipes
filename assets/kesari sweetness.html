Kesari:Sweetness in a Bowl

Description

Kasari is the perfect dessert for a delightful indulgence.Originating from south India, this popular dish is know for its rich, buttery texture and Sweetness.whether you're clebrating a festival or simple want a quick sweet fix, kesari is your go-to treat.

Ingredients

  1. Ghee-3 tbsp
  2. Cashews-10
  3. Raisins-1 tbsp
  4. Semolina
  5. water-1 cup
  6. Raw sugar-1/4 cup(quantity can be adjusted as per your taste)
  7. Saffron water-2 tbsp
  8. Cardamom powder-1/4 tsp

steps

  • First, heat up the ghee in a pan and toss in the cshews and raisins
  • Roast them on low flame until the cashews turn golden brown and the rasisins puff up.set them aside, but keep the ghee handy.
  • In the same ghee, add the semolina and roast it gently on low flame for about 5 minutes stirring continuously unit it releases a lovely, nutty aroma. once done, set it aside.
  • Now in a large pan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil. this is the base that will help the semolina cook perfectly.
  • Then, lower the flame and slowly add the roasted semolina to the boiling water.
  • Aqain stir continuously to ensure there are no lumps. let the semolina absorb all the water.
  • Keeping the flame low,add the sugar and stir it in unitil it in fully dissolves, creating a smooth sweet mixture.
  • After that, pour in the saffron water or a bit of yellow food color, giving your kesari its iconic golden hue. stir well to comubine.
  • Now,add 1/4 cup of ghee, and keep stirring to ensure the semolina blends smoothly with the ghee, whith no lumps in singht.
  • Conver the pan let it simmer for 2 minutes on low flam unit the semolina is fully cook and fluly.
  • Finally, add the roasted cashews,and raisins, and sprinkle the cardamom powedr. mix it all until the kesari pulls away from the pan.

    • Serve your kesari hot, and enjoy rich, fragrant goodness!
