Simple White Cake

Discription

This simple cake recipe was sent home from our children's school lt is the easiest,best-tasting cake l've ever made. it's great to make with the kids, especialy for cupcakes. use your frosting on top of this delicious cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.preheat the over to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). grease and flouur a 9-inch square cake
  2. Cream sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl. add egg,one at a time, beating briefly after each addition.mix in vanilla
  3. Combine flour and banking powder in a separate bowl. add to the wet ingredients and mix well. add milk and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter the prepared cake pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated over until the top spring back when lightly touched,30 to 40 minutes.
  6. Remove frome the oven and cool completely.frost and enjoy!

Recipe tip

A 10-inch round pan will produce similar results.to make cupcakes,line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners. divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups and bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
